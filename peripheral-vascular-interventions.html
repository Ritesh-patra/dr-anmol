❤️ Peripheral Vascular Interventions

Peripheral vascular diseases affect the blood vessels outside the heart and brain. We provide minimally invasive, image-guided treatments to restore blood flow, relieve symptoms, and prevent limb-threatening complications.

Limb-Saving Arterial Interventions

  • • Angioplasty & Stent Placement
  • Opens blocked arteries to improve walking distance, reduce pain, and prevent amputation.
  • • Drug-Coated Balloon / Atherectomy & Advanced Endovascular Therapies

Venous & Varicose Vein Care

  • • Targeted Therapies for Varicose & Refluxing Veins
  • Minimally invasive embolisation/ablation techniques relieve pain, swelling, heaviness and cosmetic concerns without surgical stripping.

Wound & Foot Salvage (Diabetic Patients)

  • • Endovascular Revascularisation
  • Restores circulation to help heal chronic ulcers and non-healing diabetic foot wounds, reducing the need for amputation.

Detailed Procedures

Lower Extremity Angioplasty

Treatment of peripheral artery disease preventing amputation:

  • Restores blood flow to legs
  • Relieves claudication and pain
  • Preserves limbs from amputation
  • Success rates >90%

Arteriovenous Fistula (AVF) Creation

Access creation for dialysis patients:

  • Percutaneous technique avoids surgery
  • High blood flow for dialysis
  • Lasts years with proper care
  • Lower infection rates

Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) Treatment

Management of blood clots in legs and arms:

  • Mechanical thrombectomy or thrombolysis
  • Prevents pulmonary embolism
  • Reduces post-thrombotic syndrome
  • Faster recovery than anticoagulation alone
