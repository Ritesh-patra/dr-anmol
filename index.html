Vascular Interventional Radiologist

Dr. Amol Srivastava

Dr. Amol Srivastava is a leading Interventional Radiologist dedicated to treating complex medical conditions through the smallest possible incisions. By combining diagnostic precision with advanced endovascular techniques, he offers patients a safer alternative to traditional open surgery resulting in less pain, reduced risk, and faster recovery times.

While Dr. Srivastava is frequently called upon to manage critical emergencies such as bleeding and acute vascular obstructions he maintains a special interest in outpatient "day-care" therapies that allow patients to return home the same day.

His commitment to innovation and patient-focused treatment continues to make a meaningful impact in the field of interventional radiology.
