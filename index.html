Dr. Amol Srivastava

Interventional Radiologist

Dr. Amol Srivastava

Dr. Amol Srivastava is an interventional radiologist with specialized expertise in endovascular and image-guided therapies. He manages a wide spectrum of vascular and non-vascular conditions using minimally invasive techniques that reduce patient risk, pain, and recovery time.

From peripheral and hepatobiliary interventions to oncologic and portal hypertension procedures, Dr. Srivastava combines strong diagnostic radiology training with advanced interventional skills to deliver precise, evidence-based, and patient-centered care.

His commitment to innovation and patient-focused treatment continues to make a meaningful impact in the field of interventional radiology.
