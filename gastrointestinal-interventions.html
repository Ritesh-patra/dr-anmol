Key Liver & Biliary Procedures

PTBD (Percutaneous Transhepatic Biliary Drainage)

Relieves jaundice by clearing blocked bile ducts.

Targeted therapy that delivers anti-cancer drugs or radiation directly to the tumour with minimal effect on the rest of the body.

Thermal Liver Ablations (RFA/MWA/IRE)

Destroys tumours using heat or electrical pulses without open surgery.
