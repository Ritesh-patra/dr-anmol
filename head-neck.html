Head & Neck Interventions

Advanced minimally invasive treatments for head and neck conditions.

Overview

Head and neck interventional services provide advanced, minimally invasive treatment for tumors, vascular lesions, and obstructive conditions in the head and neck region. Our specialists use sophisticated imaging and endovascular techniques to achieve optimal outcomes with minimal patient morbidity.

Specialized Procedures

Tumor Embolization

Arterial embolization to reduce tumor blood supply and facilitate treatment.

Vessel Stenting

Carotid and vertebral artery stent placement for stenosis treatment.

Biopsy Procedures

Image-guided biopsy of head and neck masses for accurate diagnosis.

Vascular Malformation Treatment

Embolization of arteriovenous malformations and hemangiomas.

Conditions We Treat

Head and neck cancers
Carotid artery stenosis
Arteriovenous malformations
Hemangiomas
Epistaxis (nosebleeds)
Vascular access complications

Advantages of Our Approach

Precision Treatment

Advanced imaging ensures accurate targeting with minimal damage to healthy tissue.

Preserved Function

Minimally invasive techniques preserve speech, swallowing, and other vital functions.

Quick Recovery

Reduced hospitalization and faster return to normal activities.
