Urological Interventions

We offer advanced, needle-/catheter-based treatments for kidney, ureter, prostate and adrenal disorders. All procedures are performed under real-time imaging to ensure accuracy, faster recovery and minimal discomfort.

Key Vascular & Renal Procedures

Renal Artery Angioplasty & Stenting

Improves blood flow to the kidneys in renal artery stenosis and renovascular hypertension.

Renal / Peri-renal Bleeding Embolisation

Stops bleeding after trauma, surgery or tumour — life-saving and organ-preserving.

Kidney Tumour Ablations (RFA / MWA / Cryoablation)

Destroys small kidney tumours without major surgery.

Ureter & Collecting System Interventions

PCN Tube Placement

(Percutaneous Nephrostomy) - Safely relieves blockage and protects kidney function.

DJ Stenting Under Imaging Guidance

Precise retrograde or antegrade stent placement in difficult cases.

Prostate & Adrenal Specialised Services

Prostatic Artery Embolization (PAE)

Non-surgical treatment for BPH/enlarged prostate — reduces urinary symptoms while preserving sexual function.

Adrenal Vein Sampling (AVS)

Gold-standard test to localize hormonal excess in primary aldosteronism before surgery.
