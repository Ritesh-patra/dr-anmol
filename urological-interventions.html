Detailed Procedures

Percutaneous Nephrolithotomy (PCNL)

Gold standard for large kidney stones:

  • Stone-free rate >95%
  • Image-guided access
  • Direct visualization and removal
  • Shorter recovery than open surgery

Ureteral Stent Placement

Relief of ureteral obstruction from stones, tumors, or strictures:

  • Preserves renal function
  • Prevents infection and sepsis
  • Temporary or long-term use
  • Can be placed endoscopically or percutaneously

Renal Mass Ablation

Minimally invasive treatment of small renal masses:

  • Radiofrequency or cryotherapy
  • Preserves kidney function
  • Outpatient or short stay procedure
  • Low complication rates
